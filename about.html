About Earth Day Celebration

Discover our mission, project background, and the technology behind our sustainable digital experiences.

Our Mission

At Earth Day Celebration, our mission is to promote environmental awareness and encourage sustainable practices through engaging digital experiences.

We believe every small effort counts towards protecting our planet and strive to inspire positive change through creative technology.

About the Project

This website combines a dynamic rain simulator and a guide for 7 sleep-inducing soups. The rain simulation is designed to provide a calming, immersive experience, while the sleep soups guide offers natural recipes inspired by traditional Chinese medicine.

The project is part of a larger effort to celebrate Earth Day and promote sustainable living through innovative, interactive web applications.

Technology Used

  • HTML5 & CSS3 for structure and design.
  • Tailwind CSS for fast, responsive, utility-first styling.
  • JavaScript for interactive features and dynamic content.
  • Three.js for the 3D rain simulation experience.

These modern technologies allow us to build engaging, responsive, and visually appealing web experiences that work smoothly on any device.
